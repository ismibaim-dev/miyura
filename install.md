# Install on your device

Miyura is a Progressive Web App (PWA). That means there's nothing to download from an app store — you visit the site, install it, and it behaves like a regular app from then on.

The whole process takes about a minute.

## On iPhone or iPad

1. Open the Miyura site in **Safari** (this only works in Safari on iOS, not Chrome or Firefox).
2. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom of the screen.
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Confirm the name, then tap **Add**.

You'll find Miyura on your home screen with its own icon. Tap it to open — it'll run full-screen, without Safari's UI.

::: tip Why Safari only?
Apple restricts PWA installation to Safari on iOS. This is a platform limitation, not a Miyura one.
:::

## On Android

1. Open the Miyura site in **Chrome**, **Edge**, or **Firefox**.
2. You should see an **Install app** banner near the address bar. If you don't, open the browser menu (⋮) and choose **Install app** or **Add to Home Screen**.
3. Confirm.

Miyura appears in your app drawer alongside your other apps.

## On desktop

Most modern browsers (Chrome, Edge, Brave, Arc) will offer to install Miyura as a desktop app. Look for an install icon in the address bar.

You can also just bookmark the site and use it that way — there's no functional difference.

## After installing

The first time you open Miyura, you'll have an empty library. Head to [Adding sources](./sources) next.

## Uninstalling

- **iOS**: long-press the icon → Remove App → Delete from Home Screen.
- **Android**: long-press the icon → Uninstall.
- **Desktop**: open the app → menu → Uninstall.

Removing the app removes your library and reading history from that device. We don't store either on a server.
